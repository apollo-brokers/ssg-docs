new.website is an AI-powered platform for building professional websites through conversation. Simply describe what you want, and our AI creates it instantly - complete with forms, content management, automation, and custom domains. No coding required, no developers needed.

## Get Started

To get started, simply put your prompt of the website you want to build at [https://new.website](https://new.website):

  <img src="/images/introduction/step-1.png" alt="new.website homepage with website creation prompt" />
</Frame>

From there, our AI will create your website in 4-8 minutes. Once complete, you can [chat with the editor](/editor) to customize and enhance your site, then add other features like forms, automation, and custom domains.

  We have a whole guide on the editor if you're curious - check it out [here](/editor).

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Website">
    Use our [AI-powered editor](/editor) to build your website by describing what you want in simple, natural language. The editor understands conversational commands and creates professional websites without any coding required.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Content and Media">
    Upload images, videos, and organize your content using our built-in [CMS](/features/cms) and [media library](/features/media). Create reusable content snippets, blog posts, and manage all your visual assets in one place.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Features">
    Set up interactive [forms](/features/forms) to collect visitor information, connect your [custom domain](/features/custom-domain) for a professional web presence, and integrate with [Zapier](/features/zapier) to automate form responses and connect to over 8,000 apps.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish and Share">
    Publish your website and share it with the world using your custom domain or our provided URL. Your site will be optimized, secure, and ready for visitors.
  </Step>
</Steps>
